Steven Dow.

Product
Designer.

I'm a Product Designer with more than 20 years of industry experience. I've collaborated with cross-functional teams around the globe designing digital products and advising Fortune 500 companies, scale-ups and corporate start-ups.


I can manage all design aspects of a project from start to finish and lead end-to-end user experience projects, ranging from single app design to enterprise-scale implementations.

My main qualities are versatility, great attention to detail, and translating information into practical product features.


I'm a designer with a broad range of interests, from UX research and UX design to UI Design and front-end development. I make sure to deliver the best results to create fully functioning products.
